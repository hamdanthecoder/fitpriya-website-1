Account and data deletion

Delete Your FitPriya Account

If you have created a FitPriya account, you can request deletion of your account and associated personal data using the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

Email xbrostudioind@gmail.com with the subject line FitPriya Account Deletion Request. Include the email address linked to your FitPriya account so we can verify the request.

What Will Be Deleted

After verification, we will delete or anonymize account data associated with your FitPriya account, including profile information, fitness goals, workout progress, nutrition logs, meal plan data, habit progress, hydration logs, sleep entries, mind challenge scores, and app progress data where stored on our servers.

What May Be Kept

Some limited information may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, dispute-resolution, billing, backup, or legitimate operational reasons. Local data on your device may remain until you uninstall the app or clear app storage.

Processing Time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within a reasonable period. If we need additional information to verify the request, we may contact you using the email address provided.

Need Help?

For any questions about account deletion or privacy, contact xbrostudioind@gmail.com.
