Privacy Policy

FitPriya is a fitness, nutrition, habit, hydration, sleep, and mind training app operated by XBro Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how FitPriya accesses, collects, uses, shares, protects, retains, and deletes user data.

Developer and Privacy Contact

FitPriya is provided by XBro Studio. For privacy questions, account deletion, or data requests, contact us at xbrostudioind@gmail.com or through the contact page.

Information We Collect

Depending on which features you use, FitPriya may collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, authentication provider, user ID, profile image or avatar initials, and account settings.
  • Fitness and wellness information: fitness goals, activity level, workout plans, exercise progress, completed workouts, skipped or resumed workouts, habit progress, hydration logs, sleep entries, mind challenge scores, and discipline/progress scores.
  • Nutrition information: meal searches, food logs, calories, macronutrients, meal plan preferences, generated meal plans, and daily nutrition progress.
  • App usage and device-related information: app settings, notification preferences, crash or diagnostic information, approximate app activity, and advertising identifiers where ads are shown.

Personal and Sensitive Data

Some fitness, nutrition, hydration, sleep, and wellness information may be considered personal or sensitive data depending on your country or region. FitPriya uses this information only to provide app features such as personalization, progress tracking, meal guidance, reminders, and wellness insights.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create, authenticate, and manage your account.
  • Personalize workouts, meal guidance, habits, reminders, and Coach Priya messages.
  • Track workout, nutrition, hydration, sleep, habit, and mind training progress.
  • Generate or improve meal plans and wellness recommendations.
  • Sync your data across app sessions and devices where supported.
  • Send reminders and notifications if you allow them.
  • Show ads to free users, limit ad frequency, and measure ad performance.
  • Improve app reliability, prevent abuse, debug issues, and keep the app secure.

Third-Party Services

FitPriya may share limited data with service providers that help operate the app. These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and our app functionality needs.

  • Firebase / Google services: authentication, sign-in, app services, and notifications.
  • Render and MongoDB: backend API hosting and database storage for app data.
  • Supabase: hosting workout exercise images or frames.
  • OpenAI or compatible AI providers: generating meal guidance or coach-style content when the feature is used.
  • Google AdMob: showing ads, measuring ads, and limiting ad frequency for free users.

Advertising

FitPriya may show ads through Google AdMob to free users. AdMob may collect or use device identifiers, approximate app activity, ad interaction data, and related technical information to deliver, limit, personalize, and measure ads. You can manage ad personalization through your device settings or Google ad settings.

AI Features

FitPriya may use AI services to generate meal plans, meal explanations, or coach messages. Information you submit for these features, such as meal preferences, goals, or food search context, may be sent to the AI provider only to produce the requested result and support app functionality.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, FitPriya may send reminders about hydration, habits, workouts, meals, motivation, and app progress. You can turn notifications off in your device settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only when needed to provide app features, operate service providers, comply with law, prevent fraud or abuse, protect users, or complete a user-requested action.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal and sensitive user data. Data may be transmitted over encrypted connections where supported by the app and service providers. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect user data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Data Retention

We keep account and app data for as long as needed to provide FitPriya, maintain your account, support progress tracking, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, and improve reliability. Some local data may remain on your device until you delete the app, clear app storage, or log out depending on the feature.

Account and Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your FitPriya account and associated personal data at any time. Visit the Account Deletion page or email xbrostudioind@gmail.com with the email address linked to your FitPriya account. After verification, we will delete or anonymize account data unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or legitimate operational reasons.

Your Choices and Controls

You can update app information, change notification preferences, manage ad personalization, stop using optional features, uninstall the app, or contact us about access, correction, deletion, or privacy questions.

Health and Fitness Notice

FitPriya provides general wellness and fitness guidance. It is not medical advice. Users should consult a qualified professional before starting a new fitness or diet routine, especially if they have a medical condition.

Children

FitPriya is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and remove it where required.

International Users

FitPriya may be operated from or hosted in countries different from where you live. By using the app, you understand that data may be processed in locations where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features, legal requirements, or data practices change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact XBro Studio at xbrostudioind@gmail.com or through the support page: Contact FitPriya.
